## Website Phone Number Scraping Tool: Intelligently and batch-crawls phone numbers from websites, improving data acquisition efficiency and providing powerful support for your business expansion.

# Features

Intelligent web crawler technology: Employing advanced web crawler technology, it automatically and deeply crawls the content of website comment sections, accurately identifying and extracting mobile phone numbers from the comments on the page.

Precise filtering function: Supports filtering by phone number prefix and setting a blacklist of title keywords, collecting only the target numbers you truly need.

Flexible data storage: Supports both local file storage and remote database storage to meet data storage needs in different scenarios.

Continuous operation mechanism: Designed for long-term idle operation, it automatically clears access record cache and periodically switches output files to ensure stable operation.


UA Simulation and Configuration: Supports customizable UA browser identifiers, effectively circumventing website anti-scraping mechanisms and improving data collection success rate.

## Commercial Applications

Market Research: Collect contact information for businesses in specific industries or regions to provide data support for market research.

Customer Development: Obtain contact information for potential customers, expand sales networks, and improve business conversion rates.

Industry Analysis: Collect contact information for websites in specific industries for industry data analysis and statistics.

Corporate Promotion: Obtain mobile phone numbers of target customers for SMS marketing or telephone promotion.
